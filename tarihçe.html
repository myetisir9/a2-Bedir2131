CLUB



22 young people worked gymnastic in 1902 some days of week in the garden of Osman Pasha's mansion. The first branches of the youth including Osman Pasha, Mehmet Şamil and Hüseyin Bereket are did gymnastics and interested in sports.In 1903 Bereket Gymnastics Club was founded so Foundations of Besiktas was laid.After time passed Bereket Gymnastics Club changed as Ottoman Gymnastics Club thanks to Muhittin Bey.It is first Turkish sport club.In these days interest in this sports club grew and the number of members engaged in sports increased to 150 at a time.



Soccer is not a one one preffed sport because of political situation and social etiquette. Even though, football was overshadowed by other sports in the early period, starting from the late 1910s, the athletes and the gymnasts of themselves. In the Club of Besiktas Club. In August 1911, Ahmet Şerafettin Bey (Şeref Bey), Chairman and Founder of Valideçeşme Football Club, joined Beşiktaş with his players. As a result of the basement, Basiret Club joined Besiktas as well. Thus, a part of the Club was started operating officially within the Club.



Therefore, Beşiktaş club has made many firsts. (- First Turkish sports club (1903)- First Turkish sports club with training facilities and headquarters (Akaretler-1909) - First Turkish sports club visited by Great Leader Atatürk (1914) - First Istanbul League champions (1924))
